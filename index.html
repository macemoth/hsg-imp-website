OICU is a context-based identification system

OICU provides explainable and robust object identification for videos, keeps track of them and stores the scene events in a temporal graph.

Situating OICU

OICU features a combination of subsymbolic and symbolic AI, harnessing the real-world performance of subsymbolic AI and the transparency of symbolic reasoning.

Features

Identification

Instead of merely classifying objects into generic classes, it creates locally unique identifiers (LUI) for every recognised object from their context. LUIs are transparent and can even be integrated in other systems.
